ACCESS CONTROL POLICY
Version: 2.1 | Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. PURPOSE
This policy establishes standards for controlling access to company systems, applications,
and data to protect against unauthorized access and data breaches.

2. SCOPE
Applies to all employees, contractors, and third-party vendors who access company systems.

3. MULTI-FACTOR AUTHENTICATION (MFA)
3.1 MFA is MANDATORY for all user accounts, especially administrative and privileged accounts.
3.2 MFA must be enforced for all remote access (VPN, cloud services, SaaS applications).
3.3 Exception requests must be approved by the CISO in writing and reviewed quarterly.
3.4 Accounts without MFA enabled are considered a HIGH-RISK compliance violation.

4. LEAST PRIVILEGE PRINCIPLE
4.1 Users must only have access permissions necessary to perform their job function.
4.2 Privileged access (admin rights) must be reviewed and re-certified every 90 days.
4.3 Access rights must be revoked within 24 hours of employee termination.
4.4 Shared accounts are strictly prohibited for all systems classified as confidential or above.

5. PASSWORD POLICY
5.1 Passwords must be at least 14 characters in length.
5.2 Passwords must include uppercase, lowercase, numeric, and special characters.
5.3 Password reuse is not permitted for the last 12 passwords.
5.4 Passwords must be changed every 90 days for privileged accounts.
5.5 Default vendor passwords must be changed immediately upon deployment.

6. ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT
6.1 All accounts must be reviewed for necessity quarterly.
6.2 Dormant accounts (inactive for 60+ days) must be disabled immediately.
6.3 Service accounts must use non-expiring passwords only when documented and approved.
6.4 All account creation and deletion events must be logged and auditable.

7. COMPLIANCE & ENFORCEMENT
7.1 Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.
7.2 Automated monitoring tools must be deployed to detect access policy violations.
7.3 Annual access control audits are required across all critical systems.
