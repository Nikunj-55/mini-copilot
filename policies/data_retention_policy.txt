DATA RETENTION POLICY
Version: 1.4 | Effective Date: March 15, 2025

1. PURPOSE
This policy defines how the company retains, archives, and disposes of data to meet
regulatory obligations, reduce liability, and protect sensitive information.

2. SCOPE
Applies to all data types across all departments including HR, Finance, Legal,
Technical, and Customer data, whether stored on-premises or in cloud environments.

3. RETENTION SCHEDULES
3.1 Customer Personal Data: Retained for the duration of the business relationship plus 3 years.
3.2 Financial Records: Retained for 7 years in accordance with tax and accounting regulations.
3.3 Employee Records: Retained for 7 years after termination of employment.
3.4 Contracts and Legal Documents: Retained for 10 years after expiration.
3.5 System Logs and Audit Trails: Retained for a minimum of 1 year, preferably 3 years.
3.6 Security Incident Records: Retained for 5 years.

4. DATA CLASSIFICATION
4.1 CONFIDENTIAL: Includes PII, financial records, health data. Encrypted at rest and in transit.
4.2 INTERNAL: Business operations data. Access limited to employees.
4.3 PUBLIC: Marketing materials and publicly available information.

5. DATA ENCRYPTION
5.1 All confidential data must be encrypted using AES-256 or equivalent at rest.
5.2 Data in transit must use TLS 1.2 or higher.
5.3 Encryption keys must be rotated annually and stored in a secure key management system.
5.4 Unencrypted storage of customer PII is a HIGH-RISK compliance violation.

6. DATA DISPOSAL
6.1 Data past its retention period must be securely deleted using DoD 5220.22-M standard.
6.2 Physical media must be shredded or degaussed before disposal.
6.3 All data disposal events must be documented with date, method, and authorizing person.
6.4 Cloud data deletion must include verification from the cloud provider.
6.5 Improper data disposal without documented approval is a MEDIUM to HIGH compliance risk.

7. DATA BREACH PROTOCOL
7.1 All suspected data breaches must be reported to the Security Team within 1 hour of discovery.
7.2 Regulatory notification (e.g., GDPR, CCPA) must occur within required legal timeframes.
7.3 Post-breach reviews must be conducted within 30 days.

8. COMPLIANCE
8.1 Annual audits must verify adherence to retention schedules.
8.2 Violations may result in disciplinary action and regulatory penalties.
